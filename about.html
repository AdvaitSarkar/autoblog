About

Hello! This blog is an experiment in using AI language models to help communicate the results of academic papers. Full disclosure: posts are generated by prompting AI language models to generate a short explanatory article for a broad audience, based on the text of an academic paper.

The final text is checked and corrected for obvious inaccuracies by a human (me), but some inaccuracies may remain. If you rely on a particular finding for your own work (e.g., you wish to build on it in your own research), please read the original paper to confirm your understanding of the finding.

Articles on this blog should not be treated as academic sources, and should not be cited in academic papers. Instead, please cite the original paper(s) referenced in the article you wish to cite.
